Digital Twin Solutions

Physics-based real-time vessel modeling for performance, noise, and emissions optimization.

Overview

SimBlue Marine's Digital Twin technology revolutionizes vessel operations by creating real-time virtual replicas of marine assets. Our comprehensive digital twin solutions integrate sensor data, operational parameters, and environmental conditions to provide actionable insights for performance optimization, predictive maintenance, and operational efficiency. By leveraging advanced machine learning algorithms and real-time data processing, we enable vessel operators to make data-driven decisions that enhance safety, reduce costs, and minimize environmental impact.

Core Capabilities

Continuous monitoring of vessel performance metrics, fuel consumption, and operational parameters with real-time data visualization and analytics.
AI-driven analysis of equipment health, predictive maintenance scheduling, and custom analytics dashboards for data-driven decision making.
Comprehensive monitoring and optimization of emissions, fuel efficiency, and environmental compliance with real-time impact assessment.
Advanced analytics for route optimization, speed adjustments, and operational efficiency improvements based on real-time conditions, historical data, and oceanographic forecasting.
Multi-objective optimization framework for underwater radiated noise and air pollution, integrating real-time vessel data, soundscape modeling, bathymetry, and fuel consumption.
  • Uses JOMOPANS-ECHO regression models for ship source noise, accounting for ship type, speed, and length.
  • Noise levels calculated in 36 third-octave bands (10 Hz to 31.5 kHz).
  • Physics-based machine learning for underwater radiated noise and fuel consumption.
  • Demonstrated up to 45% noise reduction with less than 1% increase in fuel consumption.
Reference: JOE 2024, JASA 2024
