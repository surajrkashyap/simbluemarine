Propeller Optimization

CFD-driven propeller design and retrofits for noise and fuel savings.

Overview

SimBlue Marine specializes in advanced propeller optimization using state-of-the-art Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) and machine learning techniques. Our comprehensive approach combines numerical simulations, experimental validation, and operational data to deliver optimized propeller designs that reduce fuel consumption, minimize underwater noise, and enhance overall vessel performance. With expertise in both conventional and unconventional propeller designs, we help vessel operators achieve significant operational cost savings while meeting stringent environmental regulations.

Core Capabilities

Advanced computational fluid dynamics simulations for precise performance prediction and optimization.
Comprehensive cavitation modeling and mitigation strategies for improved efficiency and reduced noise.
Accurate prediction of propeller performance across various operating conditions using validated numerical models.
Custom retrofit designs for existing vessels to improve efficiency and reduce environmental impact.
Comprehensive testing and validation of optimized designs using advanced measurement techniques.
Tailored propeller designs for specific vessel types and operational requirements.

Our Optimization Services Include

  • Complete propeller design and optimization packages
  • Retrofit solutions for existing vessels
  • Performance analysis and validation
  • Operational efficiency improvement programs
